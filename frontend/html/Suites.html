Description

Experience elevated island luxury in our beautifully appointed Suites at Celestia Bay Hotel-where elegance, space, and privacy come together to create a truly indulgent stay. Nestled in the most serene corners of the resort, each Suite offers uninterrupted views of the ocean or tropical gardens, making it the ideal sanctuary for honeymooners, families, or discerning travelers seeking refined comfort.

Our spacious Suites feature a separate living area and bedroom, designed with natural textures, modern Sri Lankan craftsmanship, and a palette of soft earth tones that reflect the calming beauty of the bay. Step onto your expansive private balcony or terrace to enjoy morning coffee, sunset cocktails, or simply soak in the gentle ocean breeze. Inside, you'll find a plush king-size bed with high-thread-count linens, a full-sized sofa, and elegant dining space- perfect for in-room meals or entertaining. A large 55-inch smart TV, Bluetooth sound system, and high-speed Wi-Fi provide entertainment and connectivity, while a stocked minibar, espresso machine, and premium welcome amenities elevate your arrival experience.

IThe ensuite bathroom is a retreat in itself, featuring a deep soaking bathtub with bay views, dual vanities, a walk-in rain shower, fluffy bathrobes, slippers, and a deluxe selection of herbal and aromatic toiletries sourced from local wellness traditions.

Guests staying in Suites enjoy exclusive benefits such as late checkout upon availability, complimentary evening turndown service, and personalized concierge assistance. Whether you're unwinding after a day of exploration or celebrating a special occasion, our Suites offer the ideal setting for relaxation and romance.

In addition to your luxurious private space, enjoy full access to Celestia Bay's 5-star amenities-including our infinity pool, full-service spa, yoga pavilion, and signature dining experiences. For those seeking something extraordinary, private chef dinners, in-suite spa treatments, and curated excursions can be arranged by request.

From the moment you arrive to your final sunset, every detail in our Suites is crafted to provide serenity, sophistication, and a sense of home-making your stay at Celestia Bay truly unforgettable.
