Our Vision
To be the leading coastal sanctuary in Sri Lanka, celebrated for blending authentic local culture with sustainable luxury, inspiring guests to reconnect with nature and themselves.
Our Story

In 2012, our founder, Amara, stood at the edge of Sri Lanka's southern coast, captivated by its untouched beauty and calming rhythm. She envisioned a place where travelers could escape the noise of everyday life and reconnect with nature, comfort, and themselves. But such a retreat didn't exist—at least not the way she imagined it.

Our Mission
Our mission is to create soulful coastal escapes that blend serene luxury, warm hospitality, and the natural beauty of Sri Lanka—offering every guest a deeply personal and unforgettable experience.

With a background in sustainable design and a passion for authentic hospitality, Amara set out to create something new. What began as a single beachfront villa has grown into Celestia Bay: a haven of barefoot luxury, cultural richness, and personalized care. From day one, our mission has been to offer more than just a stay—but a soulful, serene experience that leaves a lasting imprint.
