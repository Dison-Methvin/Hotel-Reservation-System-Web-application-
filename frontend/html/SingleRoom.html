Description

With a stay at Celestia Bay Hotel, you'll enjoy an exclusive beachfront location nestled along the tranquil shores of Sri Lanka's southern coast. This 5-star resort is just minutes from local attractions, including Coral Bay Market and the historic Lighthouse Point. Whether you're here for work or leisure, Celestia Bay offers a perfect blend of comfort, privacy, and elegance.

Make yourself at home in one of our 150 individually designed single rooms, each featuring a private balcony with sea or garden views. Rooms include modern amenities such as a minibar, a 43-inch LED TV with satellite channels, and plush bedding for a restful stay. Complimentary wired and wireless Internet access keeps you connected, while spacious work desks cater to business travelers.

Enjoy the privacy of ensuite bathrooms complete with walk-in rain showers, luxurious bathrobes, slippers, and a curated selection of natural toiletries. Additional conveniences include in-room safes, blackout curtains, daily housekeeping, and 24-hour room service for your comfort and ease.

Relax and recharge with a visit to our full-service spa, which offers holistic treatments, massage therapies, and beauty rituals inspired by local traditions. Guests can also take advantage of the outdoor infinity pool, yoga pavilion, and fully equipped fitness center. For those wanting to explore, complimentary shuttle service to nearby cultural sites and beaches is available daily.

Savor gourmet experiences at one of our three on-site restaurants, offering everything from traditional Sri Lankan cuisine to international fine dining. Unwind in the evenings with a handcrafted cocktail at our rooftop lounge, with sweeping views of the sunset over Celestia Bay.

Buffet breakfasts are served daily (available for a fee). Additional amenities include a 24-hour front desk, concierge services, boutique gift shops, laundry facilities, and on-request airport transfers. Whether you're traveling for business, leisure, or a peaceful solo retreat, Celestia Bay Hotel delivers the serenity, service, and style you deserve.
