Description

With a stay in one of our elegant Double Rooms at Celestia Bay Hotel, you'll enjoy the perfect blend of space, style, and serenity-ideal for couples, friends, or solo guests seeking a touch of luxury. Nestled within the resort's lush tropical landscape and just steps from the private beach, our Double Rooms promise a restful retreat along Sri Lanka's enchanting southern coastline.

Each of the 90 Double Rooms features either one plush king-size bed or two premium twin beds, with soft cotton linens and blackout drapes to ensure a peaceful night's sleep. Step out onto your private balcony or terrace to take in soothing garden, poolside, or partial ocean views, enhanced by the calming sound of waves nearby.

Inside, rooms are thoughtfully designed with warm coastal tones, artisanal wood furnishings, and ambient lighting. Modern amenities include a 50-inch Smart TV with international channels, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, a fully stocked minibar, and an in-room Nespresso machine for your morning brew. Climate control, a digital safe, and a spacious wardrobe add further convenience.

The sleek ensuite bathroom features a rain shower or a deep soaking tub (based on room type), natural stone finishes, deluxe bathrobes, and an exclusive selection of botanical toiletries inspired by local Ayurvedic traditions. Daily housekeeping and 24-hour room service ensure your stay is smooth and relaxing.

Whether you're here for a romantic escape or simply enjoying island time with a loved one, the Double Rooms at Celestia Bay create the perfect setting for lasting memories. As part of your stay, you'll also have full access to our world-class facilities-including the spa, fitness center, yoga pavilion, and infinity pool.

For added indulgence, enjoy priority reservations at our signature dining venues and rooftop bar, or book a candlelit dinner on your private balcony upon request. With attentive service and every detail curated for comfort, your Double Room experience at Celestia Bay Hotel is designed to exceed expectations.
